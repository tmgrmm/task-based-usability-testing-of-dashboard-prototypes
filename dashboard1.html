Task-based usability testing of dashboard prototypes

1. Relative Risk Dashboard

In the following dashboard the risk will be assessed in terms of the below listed standard epidemiological risk assessment endpoints:

  • Prevalence: Household prevalence of gastroenteritis in %
  • Incidence: 12-month incidence rate
  • Health Burden: Disability-adjusted life years lost
  • Productivity Lost: Workdays lost per household
  • Healthcare Demand: Household demand for healthcare services in %
Vulnerable Group: Relative risk is estimated for vulnerable sub-populations, including households with members older than 65 years, pregnant women, immunocompromised individuals, persons between 16 and 18 years living alone or in shared housing, persons between 19 and 25 years living alone or in shared housing, and men below 30 living alone or in shared housing.

Maps: The spatial distributions of observed risk and risk attributable to behavioural factors over the NUTS2 regions of ten EU and EEA member states (Denmark, France, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Norway, Portugal, Romania, Spain, United Kingdom) are characterised.

Task 1: In which greater region are the most workdays lost per household (productivity loss) attributable to behavioural factors? And what is the observed workdays lost in total for this region?
