Task-based usability testing of dashboard prototypes



Thank you for taking the time to do this testing!


It is a tasked-based usability study of dashboard prototypes. The whole testing is structured into 4 sections (one for every dashboard). So all you have to do is performing some standarized tasks on these dashboards. During doing this please think aloud. After every section you are pleased to fill out a usability satisfaction questionnaire as well.


During the whole testing, you are pleased to share your screen in order that your beahvior can be recorded as well as your "think-aloud inputs". If you agree to this data collection press the agree and start button below.
