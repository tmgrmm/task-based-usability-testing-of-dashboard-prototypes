3. Impact Potential Assessment Dashboard

This dashboard is about 9 interventions in which stakeholders involved in the project may be able to target some different risk factors if they change their behaviour. This impact was defined as the reduction in DALY (=Disability-adjusted life year; expressed as the number of years lost due to ill-health, disability or ealry death) attributable to behavioural risk factors for acute gastroenteritis, expressed as a total for the population of the ten countries for which the current assessment was conducted.

The Dashboard shows the maximum interventions effect that can theoretically be obtained if the intervention reacheds 100% of the target population and leads to 100% adoption of the promoted practice in every household of the target population.

Task 1: Let's go more in detail of the intervention "store eggs right for uncooked using": What is this intervention about? Which vulnerable group is most affected of it?

Task 2: Let's go more in detail of the intervention "Heat up food touched by chicken": In which country would this intervention bring the most DALY reduction? And in which it has no impact?
