4. Channels and Approaches Dashboard

This dashboard shows the result of a survey about particular risk communication content. These were structured in terms of the stage of the “user journey” (from food choice in the store to reheating of leftovers) and, depending on the stage, specific content the risk communication materials could focus on (specific foods, pathogens, production or sales locations, etc.). The participating organisations with responsibilities for risk communication towards consumers were asked to indicate which of these possible types of content they provided to consumers in their countries.

The results are presented in percent of possible types of risk communication content provided by national food safety authorities to consumers in their country.
